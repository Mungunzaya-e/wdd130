Mongolia

Mongolia is a landlocked country in East Asia, bordered by Russia and China. Known for its vast landscapes, including the Gobi Desert and steppes, it has a rich history as the center of the Mongol Empire. The capital is Ulaanbaatar, and the official language is Mongolian. It blends traditional nomadic culture with modern influences.
